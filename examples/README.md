# Example Prompts

Use these prompts to demonstrate the assistant during interviews, demos, or README screenshots.

## Chinook Database

- List all tables in the database.
- Show the first 5 albums.
- How many customers are from Canada?
- Which countries have the most customers?
- Who are the top 5 customers by total invoice amount?
- Which employee supports the most customers?
- What are the top 10 most purchased tracks?
- Which artists have the most albums?
- What is the total revenue by country?
- Which music genre generated the most revenue?
- Show monthly sales trends.

## CSV Analytics

- How many rows are in this dataset?
- Show the first 10 rows.
- What are the column names?
- Top 5 customers by revenue.
- What is the average revenue?
- Revenue by region.
- Which region has the highest sales?
- Which customer has the lowest revenue?
- Show monthly revenue trends.
- Summarize this dataset in simple business terms.
